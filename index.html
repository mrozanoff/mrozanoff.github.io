About

I grew up in a Russian household, and a French school, causing a clash of culture that didn't quite fit in with the American kids around me. My energy often ended up with computers and learning how to code. Since middle school I have been honing my passion and using it to create and learn. Nowadays my interests lie heavily in statistical arbitrage, data managment, and machine learning.

Outside of coding, I am an active outdoorsman and hiker, my biggest hike being the 2200 mile appalachian trail at eighteen years old. I spend a lot of time on my bike in the city, and enjoy many other forms of exercise and activities. I spend my non physical exertion time learning new things, such as languages or mycology.
