About

Computer Science has been a part of my life for many years now. I was first shown HTML in middle school, where I learned basic formatting and syntax. High school was similar. It was only in college that I began to take it more seriously, and got involved with Python, then C++ and SQL, and learned how to efficiently navigate this endless world. I loved it more and more, and had the wonderful oppurtunity to teach it as well! Now I meddle around with my own projects, looking for something to put my energy into.

Outside of coding, I am an active outdoorsman and hiker, my biggest hike being the 2200 mile appalachian trail at eighteen years old. I spend a lot of time on my bike in the city, and enjoy many other forms of exercise and activities. I spend my non physical exertion time learning new things, such as languages or mycology.
